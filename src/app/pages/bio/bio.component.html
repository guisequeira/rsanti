The designer as a business partner.

I’m a Brand & Digital Designer from São Paulo/Brazil but currently working in Montreal, Canada.

As a self-taught artist, I strongly believe that a designer ought to know a fair share of business, marketing and communications in order to become a valuable asset and partner for his clients. This is why I make sure to always keep my brain busy with new knowledge, technology and skill learning. My love affair with design lies in finding the best possible solution for a project to grow and find success within its market—whether it’s a startup company or a well-established brand. Currently computing as an independent designer, I’ve had the pleasure to work for major international agencies in the last 8 years, such as FCB and Publicis as well as intimate boutique design studios in Ukraine and Canada.
